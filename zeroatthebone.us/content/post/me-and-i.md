<h1>Me and I.</h1>

<pre>
When in two days time
the nurse starts the flow
of Inotuzimab
I will be more vincible
than any could imagine.
Surface proteins on each
surprised cell fly up
like mourning doves
and everyone in the clinic
will be mighty by comparison:
The motherly looking lady
waiting for her parent
will be incandescent
the way a seraph is.
I will have capillaries
of glass. This is
all known in advance.
Surrounded by the undying,
I will feel quite still,
the axis of a great wheel
that crushes the barley.
And afterwards? the one
who leaves that bed
will not be the one
who had been made
to lie there. Inotuzimab
will walk out
followed by one owing
a great and vital debt.
</pre>
